About Garuda: The Weapon

Garuda: The Weapon is an advanced cybersecurity tool designed to analyze networks, uncover vulnerabilities, and strengthen digital defenses. Leveraging the power of Nmap, this web-based port scanner transforms raw network data into a structured, visual, and accessible format.

Key Features

  • Fast & Accurate Scanning: Perform detailed port scans to identify open services on a target network.
  • Advanced Analysis Mode: Deep fingerprinting of detected services to provide security insights.
  • Customizable & Lightweight: Designed for efficient security assessments without unnecessary complexity.
  • Real-time Results: Provides instant feedback with structured data visualization for easy interpretation.
  • Optimized for Professionals & Learners: A user-friendly approach that benefits ethical hackers, security researchers, and developers alike.
